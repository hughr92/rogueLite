# Barbarian Master V1

## Overview
Primary reference sprite for barbarian class.
All future barbarian variants, animations, and armor sets must inherit from this design.

---

## Silhouette (LOCKED)
- Strong triangular upper body (wide shoulders → narrower waist)
- Large fur shoulder mass defines class identity
- Arms clearly separated from torso for animation readability
- Thick legs and boots create grounded stance
- No thin elements; all shapes are heavy and readable at small scale

---

## Proportions (LOCKED)
- Head small relative to body (~1:5 ratio)
- Large forearms and hands
- Torso is dominant visual mass
- Lower body slightly shorter but wider for stability

---

## Armor Language (LOCKED)
- Fur shoulders (primary identifier)
- Leather + iron core armor
- Chunky shapes, minimal fine detail
- No ornate decoration
- No magic/glow elements

---

## Palette (LOCKED)
- Muted reds (cloth)
- Dark browns (leather)
- Iron grey (metal)
- Bone/light fur accents
- Low saturation, strong value contrast

---

## Rendering Style (LOCKED)
- Pixel-art-inspired, clean edges
- Strong outline/edge readability
- Minimal shading (2–3 tones per material)
- No painterly blending or gradients

---

## Notes
- This sprite establishes baseline readability for gameplay scale
- Must remain readable when scaled down significantly
- All future variants must preserve silhouette first, detail second

---

## Future Constraints
- Armor variants must not break silhouette
- Animation frames must preserve proportions exactly
- Weapon handling will be layered separately where possible
